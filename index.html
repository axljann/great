About Puto

Puto cheese is a Filipino delicacy consisting of fluffy steamed rice cakes infused with a savory blend of cheese, resulting in a delightful fusion of sweet and salty flavors.

As the puto cooks, the cheese melts slightly, infusing each bite with a creamy and slightly salty flavor that contrasts beautifully with the sweetness of the cake. Puto cheese is often enjoyed as a snack or dessert, served alongside hot beverages like coffee or tea, and it's a popular choice for special occasions and celebrations in Filipino culture.
